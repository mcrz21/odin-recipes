Bacon, Lettuce & Tomato Sandwich


Description

From allrecipes.com:
"This BLT is classic: bacon, lettuce, and tomato â€” nothing fancy, just delicious."

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 tbsp of mayonnaise
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 leaves of lettuce
  • 2 slices of tomato

Steps

  1. Cook bacon in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat until evenly browned,
    about 10 minutes. Drain bacon on a paper towel-lined plate.
  2. Toast both slices of bread. Spread mayonnaise on one side (or both!)
  3. Arrange cooked bacon, lettuce, and tomato slices on one slice of bread.
    Close to make a sandwich.
  4. Enjoy!
