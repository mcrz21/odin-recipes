Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich


Description

This is a classic Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich that everyone knows and loves!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 tbsp of peanut butter (or as much as you like!)
  • 1 tbsp of jelly (or as much as you like!)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread jelly on the second slice of bread.
  4. Join both slices of bread together.
  5. Enjoy your sandwich!
