Grilled Cheese Sandwich


Description

From allrecipes.com:
"Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook
or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again."

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 slice of cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
    Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
    Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
    Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
  3. Enjoy!
